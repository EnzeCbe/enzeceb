Research
Quantum Tunneling in Enzymatic C–H Activation
In progress - Currently on hold because of the calendar.
WKB numerical simulation of proton tunneling through asymmetric Eckart barriers. Kinetic isotope effect modeling and tunneling-corrected Arrhenius analysis in Python. Mentored by Doç. Dr. Ertuğrul Karaca (Nature editor, 2× Physical Review Letters). Targeting Journal of Emerging Investigators. This research is the main skeleton piece of my whole profile and ventures. Basically the most important piece of it all because it will open the doors of quantum biology for Corlumen.
Quantum-Assisted Hurricane Forecasting
Submitted · Aug 2026
Theoretical argument for quantum reservoir computing in chaotic atmospheric modeling. References a 2026 Physical Review Letters result showing a 9-qubit processor outperforming classical neural networks on real-world weather forecasting. Submitted to Pathways to Quantum, Global Industry Challenge - July 2026. Aim: To get the idea of the quantum bit system better.

Proton Beam Therapy — Bragg Peak Modeling
Planned - Currently my main focus.
Computational modeling of Bragg peak position in heterogeneous biological tissues. Interdisciplinary collaboration with a biology researcher. Python-based Bethe-Bloch implementation targeting variable RBE modeling. This is also for making Corlumen better and seeking possibilities in particle physics.
