Research

Quantum Tunneling in Enzymatic C–H Activation
A computational model simulating proton and hydrogen tunneling through asymmetric Eckart barriers using WKB numerical integration. Produces kinetic isotope effect (KIE) curves and tunneling-corrected Arrhenius plots showing non-linear temperature dependence — the experimental signature of quantum tunneling in enzyme catalysis. Developed in Python with publication-quality figures. Mentored by Doç. Dr. Ertuğrul Karaca (Sakarya University / University of York, Nature editor).
Targeting Journal of Emerging Investigators — In progress
Quantum-Assisted Hurricane Forecasting
A theoretical argument for why quantum reservoir computing outperforms classical ensemble models in chaotic atmospheric systems. Anchored in a 2026 Physical Review Letters result showing a 9-qubit quantum processor outperforming classical neural networks on real-world weather forecasting tasks. Applied to hurricane risk modeling and its impact on vulnerable communities.
